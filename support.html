Vocamind

Vocamind is the lazy way to memorize: add a word or phrase, and the app reminds you of your own list until it sticks. If something isn’t working, start here.

Reminders not arriving?

Vocamind uses local notifications, so it must be allowed to send them. In the app, open Settings to choose how many reminders per day and when. If reminders still aren’t arriving, enable notifications for Vocamind in your device’s system settings (Settings › Notifications › Vocamind). Reminders only show entries from your own list, so add at least one word or phrase first.

Managing your words

Add words on the Words tab: type a word or phrase and tap + to save it, then tap Add details to attach a meaning, translation, or example. Reminders schedule automatically as soon as you add an entry. Archive entries you’ve mastered and restore them whenever you like.

Privacy & data

Vocamind works fully offline with no account, and all data stays on your device — uninstalling the app removes it. See our Privacy Policy for details.
